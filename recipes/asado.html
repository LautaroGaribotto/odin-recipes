Asado

"Asado de tira" or "Tira de asado"

Is a typical cut of the Argentine BBQ and it's served in most restaurants.

Ingredients

  • Raw cut of meat (Rib strips)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Step recipe

  1. Preheat the grill until putting your hand on top we can only leave it for about 7 or 9 seconds
  2. Place the rib strips on the bone side and let them cook for about 25-30 minutes, depending on the thickness of the ribs.
  3. Once you have cooked both sides, remove the meat and let it rest for about 10 minutes before cutting.
